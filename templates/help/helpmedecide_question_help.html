How many owners will your business have?

The number of owners may limit which business structure you can choose:

  • A sole trader can only have a single owner.
  • A company can have one or more owners.
  • A partnership must have between two and twenty owners.
  • A trust must have one trustee and one or more beneficiaries

Will you hold and control an asset?

A trust is where an individual or business manages an asset (estate, property or business) on behalf of beneficiaries (people who receive profits from the asset). A trust is treated like an entity for tax purposes. The trust must register for certain tax registrations including a Tax File Number (TFN).

Trusts are expensive and complex to set up and you’ll need time and the right skills to do it properly. It's best to see a qualiﬁed, licensed professional to help you understand what’s involved.
